About Me

Hi there! 👋 I'm Gagan BP, a data analyst based in Bangalore. Welcome to my corner of the web—I'm glad you're here!

My journey into data analytics began during my MCA, when I worked on a project analyzing suicide trends in India. That experience opened my eyes to the power of data to create real impact, and it set me on a path to help others make sense of their numbers.

Today, I specialize in Power BI, Python, MySQL, Excel, Tableau, and SQL. I help business owners, managers, and teams who want to turn messy data into clear, actionable insights—whether that's through dashboards, automation, or deep-dive analysis.

What motivates me? It's seeing the "aha!" moment when a client realizes how data can drive smarter decisions. I love building dashboards that not only look good, but also make complex information simple and useful.

Outside of work, you'll find me exploring Bangalore's food scene, playing chess, or tinkering with new tech tools. I believe learning never stops—I'm always looking for new ways to grow, both as an analyst and as a person.

I believe the best results come from listening first—understanding your goals, your challenges, and what really matters to you. I enjoy working side by side with clients, asking questions, and making sure we’re always moving toward something useful, not just impressive on paper. I’m always learning, whether it’s a new tool or a new way to look at a problem, and I find a lot of joy in those small “aha” moments along the way. When I’m not working, you’ll probably find me with a good cup of coffee, exploring a new part of Bangalore, or catching up with friends and family. If you’d like to chat about data, projects, or just swap stories, I’d love to hear from you.
